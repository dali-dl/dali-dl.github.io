About Me

Da Li is a Senior Research Scientist within the Machine Learning and Data Intelligence group in Samsung AI Centre Cambridge, and also Visiting Scholars at Machine Intelligence Research group of the University of Edinburgh and at CVSSP of University of Surrey.

Da's research interests span transfer learning, meta learning and semi-supervised learning.
